== Description ==

TinyUploads is a fast and simple way to get images online.  Automatically resize and upload pictures for your blog.  Choose from 3 different thumbnail sizes, write captions, re-order images, and more.  

For a full list of features, see [TinyUploads for WordPress](http://wordpress.tinyuploads.com/).

# Feature highlights #

* **Create photo galleries** - Make groups of photos you can share in a variety of ways.
* **No file limits** - Images are resized before being uploaded, so you don't have to shrink photos yourself.  Upload straight from your digital camera if you like.
* **Lightbox support** - View images full-size in your blog without having to open a new window.
* **Captions** - Add insightful commentary to your images.
* **Reorder photos** - Use a drag/drop UI to adjust the order of pictures to build a powerful photo story.
* **Image preloading** - The next image is preloaded to minimize loading time.

== Installation ==

1. Upload "tinyuploads-photo-uploader" to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. A TinyUploads button should show up in your admin sidebar.  Click it access it.
